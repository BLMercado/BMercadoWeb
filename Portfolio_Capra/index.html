Capra

Created by Brianna Mercado

The final product of my character creation course at Umass Dartmouth. I decided to work on a fish like character. The end product is a deep sea monster that stuns its prey with its horns and eats them whole with its snake like jaw. Creating this creature was something in yet out of my comfort zone. It was my first time taking a model from start to finish but I've been creating strange creatures from as long as I can remember. The whole experience was eye opening to what I want to do in life and what I am good at. Hopefully I can do more like this in the future.
