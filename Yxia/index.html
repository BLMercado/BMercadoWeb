Yxia

Created by Brianna Mercado

Created in my concept art course at Umass Dartmouth. I decided on a mage character who wanted to become stronger. After a freak accident she gained the power she desired, but caused her physical form to warp and change into a more animalistic one. This change also pushed her ideals and desires to an extreme, craving more power by any means necessary. Creating this character I didn't know what to expect or what I wanted, I know I wanted a fantasy design but not what I wanted out of it. Creating this character I looked back to a lot of my personal character concepts in rpg games and creating a character that embodies their energies of being of quote "murder hobo" quality was something that was really fun to create.
